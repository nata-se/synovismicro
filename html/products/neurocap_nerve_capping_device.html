NEUROCAP NERVE CAPPING DEVICE

NEUROCAP is a bioresorbable peripheral nerve capping device which separates and protects the nerve-end from the surrounding environment, thereby reducing the development of symptomatic neuroma.1

Rx Only. For safe and proper use of this device, refer to the Instructions for Use.

Please contact Customer Service for Instructions for Use (IFU). Call 800.510.3318 or email smca_information@baxter.com

  • Features & Benefits

  • Ordering info

Features & Benefits
  • Protective synthetic copolymer of lactide and caprolacton1
  • Resorbable: degrades at a predictable rate1
  • Controlled mechanical strength and flexibility for 10 weeks - Mechanical barrier minimizes stimulation of the nerve stump during the critical period1

INDICATIONS FOR USE:
NEUROCAP is intended to protect a peripheral nerve end and to separate the nerve from surrounding environment to reduce the development of symptomatic end-neuroma

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

There are no known contraindications
