DERMACLOSE Continuous External Tissue Expander

Reduces time to closure with no need to re-tighten or readjust

Provides delayed primary closure of full thickness wounds. Use as an alternative to split thickness skin grafts or when there are large skin deficits, such as on fasciotomies, tumor excisions, and trauma.1

Rx Only. For safe and proper use of this device, refer to the Instructions for Use. Please contact Customer Service for Instructions for Use (IFU). Call 800.510.3318 or email smca_information@baxter.com

INDICATIONS FOR USE:
The DERMACLOSE Continuous External Tissue Expander is indicated for use in reducing or assisting with the closure of full-thickness wounds of the skin.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

The DERMACLOSE Continuous External Tissue Expander should not be used on ischemic or infected tissue. It should not be used on fragile tissue at the edges of a wound.
