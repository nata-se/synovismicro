VIVOSORB POLYMER FILM

VIVOSORB is a flexible bioresorbable polymer film designed to separate opposing tissues throughout the critical healing process, minimizing undesired tissue attachments.1 The flexibility of the product facilitates positioning during surgery.

Rx Only. For safe and proper use of this device, refer to the Instructions for Use.

Please contact Customer Service for Instructions for Use (IFU). Call 800.510.3318 or email smca_information@baxter.com

  • Ordering info

Features & Benefits
  • PLC provides safe resorption and metabolization with minimal risk of inflammatory reaction
  • Maintains physical barrier between opposing sift tissue
  • Retains tensile strength up to 10 weeks: tissue separation during the critical wound healing period
  • Transparency allows for constant visualization of the entire surgical field
  • Anchorage with sutures, clips, or surgical tacks prevents migration of the device from the desired area of protection

As a solid sheet, VIVOSORB is not susceptible to the undesirable clinical manifestations associated with gelatinous and collagenous products like excessive granulation formation.

INDICATIONS FOR USE:
VIVOSORB is indicated for use as a temporary protective sheet to separate opposing soft tissues, thereby minimizing tissue attachment.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:

There are no known contraindications
