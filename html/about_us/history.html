Founded in 2000, we are dedicated to bringing the reconstructive microsurgeon advancements in products and services.

In pursuit of being “The Microsurgeons Most Trusted Resource” we are committed to the values of trust, mutual respect, high communication and a spirit of cooperation. As a company, what matters to us is:

  • Growth
  • Relationships
  • Exercising Core Values
  • Courage

The markets we service are:

  • Plastic Reconstructive / Microsurgery
  • Head & Neck
  • Hand Extremity
  • Lymphatic
  • Orthopedic / Trauma

We proudly serve the surgeons who help save and improve the quality of patients’ lives
